what is print.stl?

what is print.stl?

PRINT.STL is not just a documentary; it's an immersive journey into the captivating world of 3D printing, meticulously crafted by visionary director Max Meadows and producer Ruibo Hou. Join us as we unravel the wonders and intricacies of this groundbreaking technology, delving deep into its workings, its myriad applications, and the endless possibilities it unlocks.

Prepare to be inspired as we bring together a diverse array of voices, from renowned YouTubers and innovative creators to industry-leading CEOs. Get ready to hear firsthand insights and untold stories from the likes of James Rooke and Mike Webb of Honeybadger 3D Print and Paint, as they share their unique perspectives on the transformative power of 3D printing.

With PRINT.STL, we're not just documenting a phenomenon; we're capturing the spirit of innovation and creativity that defines the 3D printing revolution. Join us on this thrilling odyssey, where the future is printed in three dimensions.
